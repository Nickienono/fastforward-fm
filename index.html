Hoe scoor je punten?
Elke carrière is uniek. Maximaal 10 seizoenen tellen mee voor de highscore! Punten worden toegevoegd na Ronde 34.
Eindstand (na ronde 34):
🥇 1e: 1000 · 2e: 900 · 3e: 800 · 4e: 700 · 5e: 600 · 6e: 500 · 7e: 400 · 8e: 300 · 9e: 200 · 10e: 100 · 11e–18e: 0 ptn
Topscorers bonus: eigen spelers in top-10 = +10 (1e) t/m +1 (10e) ptn
Multiplier: op basis van START-team (Top 3: ×1.0 · Top 4–6: ×1.25 · Top 7–12: ×1.5 · Top 13–18: ×2.0) Every career is unique. A maximum of 10 seasons count towards your highscore! Points added after Round 34.
Final standing (after round 34):
🥇 1st: 1000 · 2nd: 900 · 3rd: 800 · 4th: 700 · 5th: 600 · 6th: 500 · 7th: 400 · 8th: 300 · 9th: 200 · 10th: 100 · 11th–18th: 0 pts
Top scorer bonus: own players in top-10 scorers = +10 (1st) to +1 (10th) pts
Multiplier: based on STARTING team (Top 3: ×1.0 · Top 4–6: ×1.25 · Top 7–12: ×1.5 · Top 13–18: ×2.0)

How to score points?
Max 10 seasons count. Points added after Round 34. Points multiplied by your STARTING team tier. Multiplier locked for entire career (Top 3: x1 · Top 4-6: x1.25 · Top 7-12: x1.5 · Top 13-18: x2).
